🎯 The SaaS Challenge

Cliky, a B2B sales automation startup, needed to build a comprehensive multitenant CRM platform from scratch. They required AI-powered workflow automation, field sales force management, real-time analytics, mobile apps, and enterprise-grade security—all while competing with established players like Salesforce and HubSpot in a crowded market.

- ⚡ How Our Forward-Deployed Engineers Helped
Competitive SaaS Strategy:
Our FDEs worked directly with Cliky's founding team for 5 months, developing go-to-market strategy, positioning against Salesforce, and building differentiated AI-powered features that competitors couldn't match.
Multitenant Architecture:
Designed and implemented scalable SaaS architecture supporting thousands of organizations with isolated data, custom workflows, and white-label capabilities for enterprise clients.
AI-First Development:
Integrated machine learning for lead scoring, automated follow-ups, intelligent workflow triggers, and predictive analytics—providing competitive advantage over traditional CRMs.
- 🛠️ What We Built
Complete CRM Platform:
React/Node.js SaaS platform with lead management, opportunity tracking, pipeline visualization, automated nurturing, and advanced sales analytics with AI-powered insights.
Mobile Sales Apps:
Native iOS/Android apps with offline capabilities, GPS tracking, meeting scheduling, document sharing, and real-time sync for field sales teams.
AI Workflow Engine:
Custom rule engine with machine learning models for lead scoring, automatic task creation, intelligent follow-up sequences, and personalized communication recommendations.
Enterprise Integration Hub:
API-first architecture with Salesforce migration tools, email marketing integration, calendar sync, and custom webhook support for enterprise clients.
- 💻 Technology Stack
Frontend & Mobile:
React with Redux, React Native for mobile apps, TypeScript, Material-UI for consistent design system
Backend & API:
Node.js with Express, GraphQL API, PostgreSQL with multitenancy, Redis for caching, JWT authentication
AI & Analytics:
Python ML services, scikit-learn for predictive models, Apache Kafka for event streaming, custom analytics engine
Infrastructure:
AWS ECS containers, RDS Multi-AZ, ElastiCache, CloudFront CDN, comprehensive monitoring with New Relic
- 📈 Measurable Business Outcomes
40% Higher Conversion Rates:
AI-powered lead scoring and automated nurturing sequences increased qualification rates and deal closure across all client organizations using the platform.
60% Shorter Sales Cycles:
Automated workflows, intelligent task prioritization, and real-time collaboration tools accelerated deals from initial contact to closed-won status.
10K+ Active Sales Users:
Platform scaled to serve thousands of sales professionals across hundreds of organizations with 99.9% uptime and consistent user satisfaction scores above 4.8/5.
Enterprise Client Success:
Successfully competed against Salesforce and HubSpot, winning enterprise contracts worth $2M+ ARR with superior AI capabilities and competitive pricing.
